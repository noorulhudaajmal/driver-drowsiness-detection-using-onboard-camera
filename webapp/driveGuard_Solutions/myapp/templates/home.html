Keeping You Alert, Every Mile of the Way.

The DriveGuard Solution provides driver monitoring system, which is a vehicle safety system to assess the driver's alertness and warn the driver if needed and eventually apply the brakes. It is powered by Computer Vision AI services that monitors driver's face.

Services we Offer

REGULAR MODEL UPDATES

Our service constantly evolves with regular model updates, ensuring that it adapts to new driving patterns and behaviors. With these updates, the system remains highly accurate, providing up-to-date monitoring to enhance road safety.

USER COMPLAINT REGISTRY

We prioritize user satisfaction by offering a comprehensive User Complaint Registry. This service allows users to easily log complaints, submit accident report(if any) ensuring that issues are addressed promptly.

EMAIL & SMS NOTIFICATION

Stay informed with our Email & SMS Notification service, which provides alerts and updates about the model updates and driver alertness reports.

REALTIME ANALYTICS

Our Real-Time Analytics service offers deep insights into driving behaviors, allowing for instant analysis of alertness levels.

Applications

COMMERCIAL FLEET

Protect your fleet and keep your drivers safe with our Driver Alertness System. Our advanced technology can detect drowsiness and alert drivers before an accident occurs, reducing the risk of collisions and increasing productivity.

PUBLIC TRANSPORT

Passenger safety is a top priority in public transportation. Our Driver Alertness System can help prevent accidents caused by driver fatigue and ensure a smooth, comfortable ride for all passengers.

PERSONAL VEHICLES

Long drives can be tiring, but our Driver Alertness System can help you stay alert and focused on the road. Whether you're driving for work or pleasure, our technology can help you arrive safely at your destination.
